Chicken & Rice

Description

Your average anabolic meal. Let's get these gainz boyz. An easy recipe for simple chicken breast over a bed of rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Choice of Rice
  • Olive Oil
  • Choice of Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook rice in a rice cooker
  2. Cut chicken breast into smaller pieces
  3. Heat pan to medium-high heat
  4. Add olive oil
  5. Add chicken and cook until moist and juicy
  6. Serve the chicken with the rice
