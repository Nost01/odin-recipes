Poutine

Description

A Canadian classic. A bed of french fries topped with cheese curds and gravy sauce. It's not hard to fuck this delicious meal up.

Ingredients

  • Potato french fries
  • Gravy sauce
  • Cheese curds

Steps

  1. Cut potatos into the shape of fries (frozen fries are fine too)
  2. Cook fries in an air fryer until crispy
  3. Place fries on a plate, add cheese curds, then pour hot gravy over everything
